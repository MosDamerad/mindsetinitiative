Wealth and resources

African people have been exploited in benefiting from their own resources like minerals, land, agriculture etc. hence a lot of poverty on the communities.

Mindset initiative advocates for fight against poverty and help communities to benefit from there resources through programs like.

Community stock ownership program

This program benefits communities in having stock shares with companies operating within their area using the resources of that particular community this involve community engagements with companies, policy makerâ€™s engagements on community stock ownership.

Local food network systems

Agriculture as the back bone for Africa, mindset initiative supports this philosophy for every vulnerable person to benefit therefore the initiative has developed local food network systems, these networks link farmers to the final consumer through local food hubs that involves community supported agriculture, community gardens, buying and selling clubs
