About Us

Mindset Initiative is an organisation which is seeking to change the mindsets of Africans, enhance growth and solidify unity among Africans by having a common positive ideology to the solutions of the ever soaring problems that have cast Africa in a rabbit hole of under development, poverty and inferiority which have been caused by various forms of mindset control.


Founded in 2018 and fully in cooperated in 2019 as accompany limited by guarantee with ten members and also looking at employing more than 50 members, mindset initiative is an organization focusing on changing the mindsets of the African people, enhance growth within communities and solidifying unity among Africans.
