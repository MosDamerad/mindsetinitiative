Welcome to

Mindset Initiative

Mindset Initiative is an organisation which is seeking to change the mindsets of Africans, enhance growth and solidify unity among Africans by having a common positive ideology to the solutions of the ever soaring problems that have cast Africa in a rabbit hole of under development, poverty and inferiority which have been caused by various forms of mindset control.

Therefore Mindset Initiative Africa is to cultivate a renewed mindset of Africans with an approach of pan Africanism, centralization, prosperity, equality, ethnicity, and commitment to truth, unity and love, which will germinate a fruit of Africans who appreciate their diversity, beauty, wealth and resources.

Our Partners

Frank Kassongo, philosophy (PhD) in development studies at University of Western Cape, researcher in public policy analysis. Kassongo believes in jobs creation as such he started a business that his employing other young graduates, and his looking forward to open other branches in various cities across Africa. He has broad knowledge of strategic management, stakeholder engagement (internal and external stakeholders), research development, strategic planning and innovative thinking. In 2020 he started an organization called Informed Youth of Africa (IYA) with the aim of bringing together selfless and dedicated young leaders who come from the different regions within the African continent that work in unity to realize the vision of the African Union (AU) of a United Africa and to promote the African Union agenda and formalizing Swahili as the main communicative language.
